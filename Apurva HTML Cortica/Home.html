About Us

CPR Healthcare Training Academy is the leading first aid training academy
in emergency care education. The courses at academy are designed for
health care professionals and for Non-health care individuals.
The training is provided to help the patient in any condition or situation.
The peculiarity to teach both single rescuer and team basic life support
skills equipped with state of art facilities.
