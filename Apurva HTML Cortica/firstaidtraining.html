First Aid Training

First Aid Training teaches essential life-saving skills, including wound care, CPR, AED use, and emergency response, enabling individuals to provide immediate care in medical emergencies.

First aid training equips individuals with the knowledge and skills to provide immediate medical assistance to someone who is injured or experiencing a medical emergency. The primary goal of first aid is to preserve life, prevent the condition from worsening, and promote recovery until professional medical help arrives. First aid is the initial care given to an injured or ill person before professional medical help arrives. It involves simple yet crucial techniques to stabilize the patient, prevent further harm, and promote recovery.
