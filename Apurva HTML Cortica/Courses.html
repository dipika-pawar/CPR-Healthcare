Courses

CPR Healthcare Training Courses provide essential first aid and emergency response skills for both healthcare and non-healthcare professionals. The courses cover CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation), AED (Automated External Defibrillator) usage, Basic Life Support (BLS), Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS), and First Aid Training. These programs are designed to equip individuals with life-saving techniques, preparing them to handle medical emergencies efficiently. The training follows American Heart Association (AHA) guidelines, ensuring high-quality education and certification for professionals and the general public.

Our Courses

First Aid Training

First Aid Training teaches essential life-saving skills, including wound care, CPR, AED usev enabling individuals to provide immediate care in medical emergencies.

CPR and AED Training

CPR and AED Training teaches life-saving skills to respond to cardiac emergencies. It covers Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation to restore breathing and heartbeat

ASHI-CPR-AED-AND-FIRST-AID-COMBO

ASHI CPR, AED, and First Aid Combo training provides essential skills for emergency response. It covers CPR techniques, AED operation.

Basic Life Support Training

The BLS training program helps students develop the knowledge, skills and the confidence to respond to a medical emergency.

ACLS Course

ACLS (Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support) Course is designed for healthcare professionals to enhance their skills in managing cardiac arrest

Airway Management Training

Airway Management Training focuses on techniques to maintain a clear and open airway in emergency situations.
